You Guessed it

Instructions

You Guessed it is a fun single player trivia game. The goal of You Guessed It, is to get 15 questions correct, miss more than 5 and you lose. You have the ability to choose between various catagorie, and in each catagory there are 10 questions. Each correct question is worth 1 point. For each wrong question, a point will be added to the "wrong question" score. Miss 5 questions in total and you lose the game.
